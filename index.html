About LearnStyle+

LearnStyle+.com is a website that can identify your learning style by answering 25 questions. It will also recommend best ways to assess your results!

Made by students of Emilio Aguinaldo College-Manila, 12 STEM 6, Group Technology 1, this project aims to see the validity of this website in identifying students'—specifically from EAC-Manila—Cognitive Learning Style.
