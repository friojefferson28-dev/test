ABOUT US

LearnStyle+.com is a website that can identify your learning style by answering 25 questions. It will also recommend best ways to assess your results!

LearnStyle+.com is made by the students of Emilio Aguinaldo College-Manila, 12 STEM 6, Group Technology 1. It aims to see the validity of this website in identifying students'— specifically from Emilio Aguinaldo College-Manila— Cognitive Learning Style.
